Camelot Expands into South Florida

Camelot Property Management Services Corp. begins its first North Miami condominium management engagement at Three Horizons East Condominium.

Three Horizons East Condominium | 12500 NE 15th Avenue, North Miami, FL 33161

Announcement

Camelot Property Management Services Corp. is proud to announce its first residential management engagement in North Miami, Florida, at Three Horizons East Condominium, an established 89-unit condominium community located at 12500 NE 15th Avenue.

This milestone represents a strategic expansion for Camelot as we bring our high-touch, operationally focused management approach into the South Florida market. The engagement is being handled in coordination with the court-appointed receiver, the Association, past board leadership, counsel, unit owners, local consultants, and a Florida CAM-licensed affiliate partner.

Camelot's role is to help stabilize operations, rebuild financial controls, coordinate vendors and consultants, support compliance and capital planning, and create a disciplined reporting cadence for the property's recovery and long-term health.
