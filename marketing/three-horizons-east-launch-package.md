## Press Release / Website Post

### Camelot Property Management Expands into North Miami, Florida

#### Three Horizons East Condominium marks Camelot's first South Florida condominium management engagement.

Camelot Property Management Services Corp. is proud to announce its first residential management engagement in North Miami, Florida, at Three Horizons East Condominium, an established 89-unit condominium community located at 12500 NE 15th Avenue.

This milestone represents an important strategic expansion for Camelot as we bring our high-touch, operationally focused management approach into the South Florida market. The engagement is being handled in coordination with the court-appointed receiver, the Association, past board leadership, counsel, unit owners, local consultants, and a Florida CAM-licensed affiliate partner.

Built in 1972, Three Horizons East is a six-story condominium community with gated access, a swimming pool, common areas, elevator access, parking, and residential amenities that support everyday community life. The property is located in North Miami near major transportation corridors, retail, dining, fitness, parks, Miami airports, Biscayne Bay, and area beaches.

Camelot's role is to help stabilize operations, rebuild financial controls, coordinate vendors and consultants, support compliance and capital planning, and create a disciplined reporting cadence for the property's recovery and long-term health. The assignment includes practical support around budget stabilization, collections, vendor accountability, repair and maintenance planning, capital improvements, insurance requirements, life-safety coordination, 40-year recertification support, and project-management oversight.

Three Horizons East also gives Camelot a meaningful first platform in South Florida. Camelot has been preparing for a thoughtful expansion into the Florida market and is building local operating relationships with property managers, engineers, maintenance providers, guard service providers, cleaning vendors, general contractors, and other service professionals who can support condominium communities in the region.

"Camelot sees strong long-term value in established communities like Three Horizons East: resident ownership, a real physical asset, a strong North Miami location, and the opportunity to professionalize operations through practical management, clear reporting, and accountable service delivery," said David A. Goldoff, Founder and President of Camelot Property Management Services Corp.

Camelot's management philosophy combines personalized service with institutional-level systems and accountability. Our objective is to help communities operate more efficiently, improve resident satisfaction, strengthen governance, and support long-term property value preservation.

Our focus includes:

- Stronger financial controls and reporting
- Proactive building operations and maintenance oversight
- Enhanced communication with ownership and residents
- Vendor and project management accountability
- Resident-facing technology and streamlined service delivery
- Compliance coordination and capital planning
- Support for refinance readiness after stabilization

This engagement marks the beginning of Camelot's Florida presence and reflects our continued commitment to thoughtful growth, operational excellence, and building strong residential communities.
