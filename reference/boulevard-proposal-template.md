# Boulevard Tenants Corp — Gold Standard Proposal Template
## The definitive Camelot proposal format (October 2025)

## Structure:
1. **Header** — Building name, "PROPOSAL OF PROPERTY MANAGEMENT SERVICES", Prepared by Camelot
2. **Cover letter** — To Board, c/o contact, Re: Management Proposal & Scope, Dear [Name], warm opening, David sign-off
3. **Our Commitment** — Mission statement, boutique, hands-on, white-glove
4. **Dedicated Management Team** — Named individuals:
   - Property Manager (named)
   - Facilities Manager (named + visit frequency)
   - Board Support (named, Director of Condos & Co-ops)
   - Accounting (named account rep)
   - Project Management (named, licensed CM)
   - Engineering Support (named firm)
   - CPA Coordination (named)
   - Insurance & Mortgage Services (named brokerages + firm)
5. **Scope of Services & Value Proposition** — Long bullet list (15+ items)
6. **Financial Terms** — Base fee, units, ancillary ref to Schedule A
7. **Transition Plan & Start Date** — Proposed start date, first 90 days focus
8. **Closing** — Appreciation, confidence, looking forward
9. **Phase I: Operational Turnaround** — 6-point scope
10. **90-120 Day Rollout** — 3 phases with week ranges:
    - Phase I: Transition & Assessment (Weeks 1-4)
    - Phase II: Stabilization & Training (Weeks 5-8)
    - Phase III: Optimization & Reporting (Weeks 9-12)
11. **Included Services** summary
12. **Core Management Duties** — 9-point list
13. **Operational Turnaround & Standardization** — post-stabilization note
14. **Management Philosophy & Approach** — Company description + specific pain points
15. **Staff Training & Oversight** — SOPs, checklists, house rules, tech
16. **Financial Management** — Dual ledger, budgeting, soft audit, annual audits
17. **Project & Compliance Management** — LL97, legal, energy
18. **Communication & Governance** — Board meetings, resident comms, transition checklist
19. **Comparable Experience** — Similar transitions, case studies available
20. **Why Camelot is the Right Fit** — 4 bullets (Hands-On, Specialized, Proactive, Tailored)
21. **Acceptance/Signature** — Dual signature blocks
22. **Footer** — Refer to management agreement for terms

## Key elements:
- Named team members (not generic "1 PM, 1 Admin")
- Specific pain points of THIS building addressed in Philosophy section
- BankUnited mentioned by name
- AI meeting software mentioned as complimentary
- "White-glove" language
- 90-120 day rollout (not 60-day)
- Phase structure with WEEK ranges
- Dual ledger accounting mentioned
- Soft audit & sponsor recovery
- "Case studies available upon request"
- Personal involvement of David Goldoff in transitions
