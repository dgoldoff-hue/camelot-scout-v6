### Structure
- Article I: Definitions
- Article II: Term (2-year initial, auto-renew 1-year, 90-day notice, 4% annual increase)

- Article IV: Termination (cause 15 biz days cure, insolvency, without cause 90/60 days, 3-month termination fee)

- Article VII: Additional Services (lease, transfer, financing 1%, hearings, audit, pre-occupation, emergency)
- Article VIII: Additional Fees (application review, regulatory filings, payroll, commissions)
- Article IX: Protection of PM Company
- Article X: Indemnification (mutual with gross negligence carve-out)
- Article XI: Authority ($2,500 threshold for prior approval)
- Article XII: Bank Accounts (separate FDIC, $10K transfer approval, security deposit escrow per RPL §7-103)

- Article XV: Governing Law (NY, New York County)
- Article XVI: Entire Agreement
